ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Mert! I am an Industrial Engineer and I have a M.Sc. in International Trade and Finance. I have nearly 5 years of experience as Industrial Engineer.

I think commanding a non-living thing whether for fun or for a task is amazing. I love doing hard or repetetive boring tasks with programs. With just a little bit adjustment, computers can solve our problems and do all the boring stuff for us.

I used programming for problems that I encountered in my career. I developed little programs to automate daily tasks. After a while I realised most exciting parts of my career was those moments when I create programs. So, I decided to change my career to tech.

I'm a self thougt developer with a passion for web development and mobile app development.
