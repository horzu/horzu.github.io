ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Mert! I am an Industrial Engineer and I have a M.Sc. in International Trade and Finance. I have more than 5 years of experience as Industrial Engineer.

I used programming for problems that I encountered in my career. I developed little programs to automate daily tasks. After a while I realised most exciting parts of my career was those moments when I create programs. So, I decided to change my career to tech.

I'm a self-taught front-end developer with passion for web development and mobile app development. I enjoy building responsive, pixel perfect websites with proper UI/UX.
